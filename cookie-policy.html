Bargund Consulting Cookie Policy

Bargund Consulting uses cookies on this website. By use of this site, you accept the use of cookies in accordance with this Cookie Policy. You may disable use of cookies on this site through your browser settings.

Overview of Cookies

Cookies are small files that are downloaded by a browser when a set is visited. Cookies are returned to an originating site and/or third party site on subsequent visits. Cookies may serve several functions, including remembering user preferences, visited pages, time and dates, and other metrics.

Types of Cookies Placed on the Site

Bargund Consulting's uses first-party cookies (small text files that Bargund Consulting stores locally on your computer) on Client Sites for one or more of the following purposes:

1. User preference storage
2. A/B performance testing
3. Usage pattern assessment and traffic analysis
4. Advertisement performance assessment
5. Cookies placed by Bargund Consulting do not include personally identifiable information such as name, phone number, e-mail address or mailing address, nor does Bargund Consulting link cookies to such personally identifiable information in our servers or databases.

To Block or Remove Cookies

If you would like to block, remove, or opt-out of the placement of cookies by Bargund Consulting, please see the instructions on our "Opt Out page".

Further Information
