Maven Consulting Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 19, 2019

Maven Consulting is dedicated to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes the privacy practices of Maven Consulting, LLC. (“Maven Consulting”, “we”, “us”, “our”) on our websites located at the domains maven-consulting.org, as well as such other websites or mobile applications that we own or operate (individually and collectively, “Our Website” or the “Maven Consulting Website”). Contact us if you have any questions about the content below.

What information do We collect?

We collect information from you when you visit our website. When submitting or registering on our website, you may be asked to enter your: name, e-mail address, and other personal information. You may, however, visit our website anonymously.
Device Information from Maven Consulting Visitors
We may receive and collect certain Information furnished by the visitor's browser or otherwise by their access device (“Device Information”), such as:
1. Browser Information
2. Operating System Information
3. Mobile Device Information (e.g., device identifier, mobile operating system, etc.)
4. IP address (stored in anonymized form)
5. Page Accessed
6. Geographic Location
7. Time of visit (by day, week, and duration)
8. Whether the visitor is a new or return visitor
Other such Information as may be delivered or shared by the applicable browser or access software or device.

Referral Information
We may also receive certain information regarding visitors from the prior website visited by such visitors (“Referral Information”). For instance, If a Maven Consulting Visitor navigates to the Maven Consulting Website from another website, Maven Consulting may receive certain information from the referring website (i.e., the last website the visitor visited before visiting the Maven Consulting Website) as part of the Hypertext Transfer Protocol ("HTTP") request. For example, if a Maven Consulting Visitor navigates to the Maven Consulting Website from a search engine, Maven Consulting may receive information contained in the referring URL for the applicable search query, such as the terms the Maven Consulting Visitor was searching for before visiting the Maven Consulting Website.

What do We use your information for?

Any of the information We collect from you may be used in one of the following ways:

  • To improve our website
  • To improve customer service

How do we protect your information?

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information when you place an order or enter, submit, or access your personal information. One of the ways we do this is through SSL encryption--the lock in the upper left-hand side of the URL--to secure any data that passes through our website.

Cookies

Cookies are small files that a site or its service provider transfers to your computer's hard drive through your Web browser (if you allow) that enables the sites or service providers systems to recognize your browser and capture and remember certain information.

We use cookies to understand and save your preferences for future visits. As described in our Cookie Policy, the Service may utilize first-party cookies (small text files that Maven Consulting stores locally on a Client Site Visitor’s device) on Client Sites for one or more of the following purposes: to help identify whether a Client Site Visitor is a first time visitor, or has visited previously (provided that we will not store identifiers that can be used to personally identify such Client Site Visitors); assess usage patterns and perform traffic analysis; identify preferences; diagnose problems with our Service; analyze trends; and otherwise administer Maven Consulting products and services. Cookies placed by Maven Consulting on the computers of Client Site Visitors do not include Personally Identifiable Information such as name, phone number, e-mail address or mailing address, nor does Maven Consulting link cookies to such Personally Identifiable Information on our servers or databases.

Online Privacy Policy Only

This online privacy policy applies only to information collected through our website and not to information collected offline.

Changes to the Privacy Policy

If we decide to change the privacy policy, we will post those changes on this page.

If you have any questions and suggestions regarding the Privacy Policy Statement, please use our Contact form to email us and we will get back to you within 24 hours.

Maven Consulting Does Not Collect Visitor Information Across Multiple Sites

Maven Consulting does not collect or aggregate information about visitor behavior on multiple, different websites.

Maven Consulting Disclaimers Regarding Personally Identifying Information of Client Site Visitors

Maven Consulting's Service is not designed to knowingly collect or extract from Client Visitor Information (including any Recorded Interactions or Device Information received from Client Sites through the Service) information that could be used to personally identify Client Site Visitors, such as a Client Site Visitor’s name, address or phone number ("Personally Identifiable Information") when a Client Site Visitor visits a Client Site that uses the Maven Consulting Service. In particular, Maven Consulting does not attempt to identify, decipher, access, or associate Personally Identifying Information regarding a Client Site Visitor from any Recorded Interactions or Device Information, provided that Maven Consulting may receive Referral Information.

Policy Concerning Children Under Age of 16

Maven Consulting does not knowingly or intentionally collect Personally Identifiable Information from children under 16. If we learn that we have Personally Identifiable Information of a child under age 16, we will remove it. If you believe we have Personally Identifiable Information about a child under the age of 16, please notify us.
