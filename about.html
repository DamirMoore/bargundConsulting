About Us

Do you need a web presence as a business? One of the main reasons we are so passionate about the internet is that is has made more millionaires and billionaires than almost any other thing. It's competing with behemoths like real estate investing. There's no doubt it has opened doors for people who have the grit and merit to succeed. Average businesses have become great businesses by using the power of the the internet. Small companies are competing with mega corporations in a classic David in Galiath battle. Something which is almost unheard of before the creation of the World Wide Web. So, do you need a web presence as a business? No. But the companies that neglect or forfeit a web presence are certainly leaving money on the table.

We believe that a company and website are more than a completed checklist. That's why Bargund Consulting places a high importance on building an identity and soul for your business. Whether that be with digital marketing or the creation of a website. As human beings, we want to do business with companies that share our beliefs and values. This is why Apple has a cult following. They embody a set of values that certain people—both customers and employees—identify with. Steve Jobs putting power in the hands of the individuals who think different. A couple of men sitting in their garage revolting against well established multi-million dollar corporations, and winning. Combine that with a quality product and website, and you have a compelling business.

Now we're going to tell you something that other web developement companies won't tell you—we are NOT the best in the industry. No matter what you do in life, there is someone, somewhere, that does it better. But we can promise you that we strive to improve and become better than we were yesterday. We do what we do because we enjoy it and we love the challenge. And at the end of the day, everyone is just competing with themselves.

The 8 Bargund Values

Be Loyal

It doesn't matter if you're the CEO or an intern. Lookout for your team. Show loyalty to your tribe.

Value Family

Family is the most important thing in the world. Don't put other things above them.

Take Responsibility

Do not attempt to blame others for a problem. Take responsibility and work together to makes things right.

Communicate Better

Good communication is essential to any organization. Clearly communicate your vision to those around you.

Be Transparent

Do not bend the truth, or leave out important details. Be upfront with people.

Empower Others

Lift others up around you, so we can achieve together, what none of us can achieve alone.

Be a Leader

Being a leader has nothing to do with your job title. Be courageous and seize every opportunity to make positive change.

Don't Settle

The worst thing is to discover a life you truely want, only after you've spent your entire life living one you didn't. Don't settle.
